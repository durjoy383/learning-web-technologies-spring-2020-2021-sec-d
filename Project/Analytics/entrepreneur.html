ELIGIBILTY

  • New entrepreneur (as per Bangladesh Bank's definition) maximum age 45 years.
  • Valid Trade License.

LOAN LIMIT

  • Term loan without collateral Tk 50 thousand to Tk 10 lac for new entrepreneur.
  • Term loan with collateral above Tk. 10 lac to Tk. 25 lac for new Entrepreneurs.

KEY FEATURES

  • For unsecured three (3) personal guarantors acceptable to the Bank and society.
  • Eligible entities: Sole Proprietorship Firms, Partnership Firms, Private limited Companies
  • For secured limit resident / commercial / industrial property/ en-cashable securities are accepted collaterals.
